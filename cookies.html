Cookie Policy

How and why we use cookies and similar technologies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We also use similar technologies like local storage for preferences.

How we use them

  • Essential: login/session management, security, load balancing.
  • Preferences: remember choices like time zone and notification settings.
  • Analytics: understand feature usage to improve Zivvy (with privacy safeguards).

Managing cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings and within the app. Disabling essential cookies may break core functionality.

Third‑party cookies

If we embed content or use analytics, those providers may set their own cookies. We aim to minimize third‑party tracking and use privacy‑protective defaults.
