Security & Data Practices

How we protect your data and keep Zivvy reliable.

Data protection

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Hardened cloud infrastructure and backups.
  • Role‑based access and least‑privilege.
  • Audit logging and anomaly monitoring.

Your controls

  • Export or delete your data on request.
  • Disable notifications anytime.
  • Remove child profiles or notes you added.

Responsible disclosure

If you believe you’ve found a security issue, email security@zivvy.app. Please include a detailed description and steps to reproduce. We appreciate coordinated disclosure.
