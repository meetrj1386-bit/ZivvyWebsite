Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to inclusive, accessible design.

Zivvy aims to meet WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines. We continue to improve accessibility as an ongoing effort.

What we do

  • Support screen readers and keyboard navigation.
  • Maintain sufficient color contrast and scalable text.
  • Provide descriptive labels for controls.
  • Avoid flashing content and provide motion‑reduced modes where possible.

Feedback

If you experience barriers, contact access@zivvy.app with details about the issue and your device/browser. We’ll work to fix it promptly.
